Our Products

Twisted Sparkles is a 5% alcohol beverage with only 3 grams of sugar, 3 grams of carbs and 100 calories in every 300ml can. All this without compromising on taste so you don’t have to feel guilty about having a good time.

Currently available in 2 flavours, passion fruit and lemon & lime.

Aluminium cans are 100% and infinitely recyclable. They are also the most recycled substrate in South Africa with an estimated recovery rate of 76.8%
